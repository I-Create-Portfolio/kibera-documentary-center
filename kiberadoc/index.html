EDUCATION

First and foremost, knowledge sharing and career development is at the core of Kibera Documentary Center activities. The primary idea of initiating the project was to impart basic to advanced photojournalistic skills to young visual storytellers and creators in Kibera. We believe that through the awareness and knowledge of constructive storytelling, we are able to tell our stories even better. Engage with with us through the wide variety of training programs and photography resources offered at KDC. We have no shortage of artists or storytellers. We have an overwhelming amount of enthuasistic storytellers. It is the economic and political realities—and the lack of resources that disconnect our stories from each other and from the world. KDC is all about availing the necessary toolkit to equip the next generation of storytellers.
