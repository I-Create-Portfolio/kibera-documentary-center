SUPPORT THE KIBERA DOCUMENTARY CENTER

KDC relies on your donations to amplify the voices of artists and thinkers who make a difference in the community. Our activities are currently primarily run through volunteering and good faith from people around us who are willing to share their expertise, time and resources to keep our programs going. Our donation plans are geared towards having full resources in the next few years. Our main aim is to have more space due to the rapid growth around some of our programs. Some of the enthuasistic upcoming storytellers lack tools to effectively carry out their projects. Other advanced trainees lack funds to carry out their projects. Either way, the little donation you may have is very much welcome.

Volunteer at the KDC

As a non-profit organization, Kibera Documentary Center relies heavily on a team of volunteers to provide support for our public programs.

INTERNSHIPS

Kibera Documentary Center has all seasons internship opportunities for everyone, locally and internationally. We seek intern volunteers with a passion for documentary photography, film, and new media arts in addition to a strong desire for social change.

Support is needed in preparing programmes and a variety of administrative tasks.

• Writing and research skills
• Familiarity with any Adobe Suite, Google Drive, Excel, Microsoft Word, and Filezilla
• Comfortable (or with assistance) doing on-the-ground outreach in and around the Kibera community
• Curriculum building
• Comfortable shooting and editing photo and video
• Experience in gallery, museum or clerical settings
• Knowledge of Swahili is a plus
• Youth mentorship skils is a plus too!

Interested intern volunteers applicants may contact us at internship@kiberadoc.org with a cover letter (please send attached as PDFs).
