EDUCATION

First and foremost, knowledge sharing and career development is at the core of Kibera Documentary Center activities.

The primary idea of initiating the project was to impart basic to advanced photojournalistic skills to young visual storytellers and creators in Kibera. We believe that through the awareness and knowledge of constructive storytelling, we are able to tell our stories even better.

Engage with with us through the wide variety of training programs and photography resources offered at KDC.

NEWS AND ARCHIVE

CAPTIONING, AND NEWS PHOTO REPORTING
Simon Valmery, AFP Journalist

Agence France Presse' journalist, Simon Valmery, takes the young photojournalists through the news reporting process. This includes news gathering and packaging, and how to communicate while on the field reporting flashing news.

FINDING YOUR STORIES 'Workshop'
Photographer and educator Bill Crandall (USA)

With the decline of traditional media, the oversaturation of images online, and the rise of AI and disinformation, the role of the ‘concerned photographer’ is shifting as well. How can photographers sustain their ‘truth-teller’ status in a world.

EVERYDAY LIFE EXHIBITON
Jeremy Onyango

Jeremy Onyango got to exhibit his personal project titled 'SASA WAKATI ULE'. He is setlled now and putting more focus on personal photography projects.

PHOTOGRAPHY SHOW & EXHIBITION NAIROBI NATIONAL MUSEUM
Everyday Kibera Collective, Nikon Kenya and African Union

In collaboration with Nikon Kenya, the European Union, and the Angolan High Commision we managed to hold an exhibition themed 'Africa Pride'- at the United Nations headquaters in Nairobi. Our group of students are among those who exhibited at the show.

OFFICIAL LAUNCH - KIBERA DOCUMENTARY CENTER
FRIDAY 21 JANUARY 2024

We have an overwhelming amount of enthuasistic storytellers. It is the economic and political realities—and the lack of resources that disconnect our stories from each other and from the world.

KDC is all about availing the necessary toolkit to equip the next generation of storytellers, we aim to grow to be able to accomodate everyone.

This is the official launch of the Kibera Documentary Center's atciivities in order to be recognized by the Kenyan education officials and be initiated to a fully fledged multimedia community training institution.
